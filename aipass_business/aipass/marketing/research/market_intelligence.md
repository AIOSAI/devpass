# Market Intelligence Framework

*Systematic approach to understanding markets, competitors, and customer needs*

## Research Categories

### Competitor Analysis
- **Direct Competitors**: Tools/platforms solving similar problems
- **Indirect Competitors**: Alternative solutions customers might choose
- **Emerging Players**: New entrants and disruptors
- **Feature Comparison**: What they do well/poorly
- **Pricing Analysis**: Models and positioning

### Customer Discovery
- **User Personas**: Detailed profiles of target users
- **Pain Points**: Real problems people face
- **Current Solutions**: What they use now and why
- **Decision Factors**: What influences their choices
- **Success Metrics**: How they measure value

### Market Trends
- **Technology Shifts**: Emerging tech affecting our space
- **Behavioral Changes**: How user needs evolve
- **Industry Evolution**: Where the market is heading
- **Opportunity Gaps**: Unmet needs and underserved segments

## Research Methods

### Primary Research
- **Customer Interviews**: Direct user feedback
- **Surveys**: Quantitative user data
- **Beta Testing**: Product validation
- **Community Engagement**: Forums, Discord, Reddit insights

### Secondary Research
- **Industry Reports**: Market size and trends
- **Competitor Content**: Marketing messaging analysis
- **Social Listening**: What people say online
- **Tool Reviews**: User feedback on existing solutions
